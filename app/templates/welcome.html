What is zymurgy anyway?
  • Last modern word of the standard English language
  • Origin: Greek zymo- (to leaven like bread, which uses yeast to do this) + -ourgia (working)
  • basically it means "working with yeast" - the science of fermentation - brewing and winemaking!
  • A lot of fun!
  • Something you should learn how to do!
    • *zymo is also the root of "enzyme", and that topic is really important for brewing!
